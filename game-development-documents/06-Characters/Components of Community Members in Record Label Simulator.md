### **`Components of Community Members in Record Label Simulator`**

#### **`1. Identity and Demographics`**

* **`Unique ID`**`: Identifies each community member.`  
* **`Age`**`: Tracked and affects roles and behavior.`  
* **`Birth Date`**`: Initial date of creation or immigration.`  
* **`Age Group`**`: Categorized into 30 groups, each spanning 4 years.`  
* **`Death Probability`**`: Increases with age, reaching nearly 100% at age 120.`

#### **`2. Roles and Occupations`**

* **`Childhood and Adolescence (0-19 years)`**`: Primarily consumers.`  
* **`Early Adulthood (20-23 years)`**`: Start seeking employment; chance to become creators.`  
* **`Mid to Late Adulthood`**`: Engage in various occupations (shopping center, factory, broadcast corporation).`  
* **`Senior Years (116-119 years)`**`: Remain in residences until passing away.`

#### **`3. Employment and Productivity`**

* **`Occupations`**`:`  
  * **`Shopping Center Personnel`**`: Assist in selling content.`  
  * **`Factory Personnel`**`: Work to produce content.`  
  * **`Broadcast Corporation Personnel`**`: Present content to enhance broadcast quality.`  
* **`Transition to Creators`**`: Potential for young members to become creators if there is a shortage.`

#### **`4. Behavior and Interaction`**

* **`Movement`**`: Speed based on wealth level (low: fast, medium: normal, high: slow).`  
* **`Content Consumption`**`: Engage with music based on active hours (nocturnal: 6 PM - 6 AM, diurnal: 6 AM - 6 PM).`

#### **`5. Community and Social Dynamics`**

* **`Communities`**`: Segmented by music genre preferences (45 unique content genres).`  
* **`Community Chunk`**`: Groups of 1,000 members, with collective behavior.`  
* **`Active Hours`**`: Diurnal or nocturnal engagement.`

#### **`6. Housing and Spawning`**

* **`Residence Allocation`**`: Members can only spawn if there is room in residential structures.`  
* **`Despawn Mechanism`**`: When inside structures, represented as numbers in the UI.`

#### **`7. Lifespan and Aging`**

* **`Lifecycle Tracking`**`: Aging progresses based on in-game time.`  
* **`Mortality Rates`**`: Increases every four (4) years starting from age 20-23 at a 3% rate.`

#### **`8. Economic Impact`**

* **`Wealth Level`**`: Influences speed and economic behavior.`  
* **`Consumption Patterns`**`: Affected by economic status and active hours.`

#### **`9. Preferences and Engagement`**

* **`Preferred content Genres and Moods`**`: Influence their interaction with music content.`  
* **`Audience Mechanics`**`: Binary engagement based on active hours for MVP.`
