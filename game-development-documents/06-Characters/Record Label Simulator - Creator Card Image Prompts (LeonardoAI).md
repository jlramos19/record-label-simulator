Record Label Simulator — Creator Card Image Prompts (Leonardo.AI)

### **Purpose**

Prompt recipes for Creator card imagery in a futuristic aesthetic, with clear controls for age, attire, finish, and color. These guide visual consistency for simulation assets.

### **Style controls**

* Age brackets: child · teen · young adult · adult · middle age · older adult

* Aesthetic: futuristic (default)

* Attire modes: regal · casual · nature

* Finish: glossy (younger) · matte (older)

* Color sets:

  * red (pink, crimson)

  * orange (yellow, brown)

  * green (light, dark)

  * magenta (purple, violet)

  * cyan (Freedom theme accents)

### **Color semantics (canon)**

* Nation cues are primary and come from silhouette/props/lighting style (tech/neon vs bioluminescent flora vs gem reflection).
* Bytenza uses blue for nation accents; do not use cyan for nation cues.
* Freedom theme uses cyan accents/motifs.
* Safe alignment uses white accents/motifs.
* Colors are layered, not exclusive (blue + cyan + white can coexist).
* Crops: head-and-shoulders, centered on face, keep nation/theme/alignment accents in frame.

### **Mixed-background examples**

Western–Native American · East Asian–Middle Eastern · African–Latin American

(Phrase as “X–Y mixed,” avoid stereotypes. Keep minors fully clothed in neutral contexts.)

### **Prompt template**

“A \[demeanor\] and \[alignment\] \[age\]-year-old \[two ethnicities\] mixed \[gender\]. \[Pronoun\] wears \[clothing\] and \[hair length\] \[hairstyle\]. \[Pronoun\] is a(n) \[profession\] in a \[place\] \[connector\] \[theme\].”

* demeanor: calm, ambitious, inquisitive, rebellious, poised

* alignment: Safe · Neutral · Risky (Alignment)

* connector: with, bathed in, framed by, evoking

* theme: lighting palette, mood motif, environment style
