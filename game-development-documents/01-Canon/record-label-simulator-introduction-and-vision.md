`Record Label Simulator — Introduction and Vision`

## **`Game Overview`**

`Record Label Simulator is a 3D tycoon simulation set on Gaia. The player assumes the role of CEO (role) with the Music Executive occupation, leading a Record Label that operates across three nations: Annglora, Bytenza, and Crowlya. The goal is to achieve Iconic Eras by managing Creators, overseeing content creation, promotion, audience alignment, and rival label dynamics.`

## **`Core Gameplay Concepts`**

* `Content Creation: refine Tracks and Projects through Themes and Moods, coordinated by Creator occupations such as Songwriter, Recorder, and Producer.`

* `Promotion: leverage Promoter occupation actions, live shows, eyeriSocial, and promotional campaigns.`

* `Economic Management: lease or own studios, set campaign budgets, and manage financial sustainability.`

* `Era Planning: each Era progresses through four stages — Direction, Creation, Promotion, Legacy.`

## **`Gaia Nations`**

`Gaia’s cultural landscape is defined by three countries:`

* `Annglora: reflective and policy-driven, favoring Safe alignments.`

* `Bytenza: precision- and innovation-oriented, aligned with Ambition.`

* `Crowlya: bold and mystical, aligned with Power and embracing Risky strategies.`

## **`Player Role`**

`The player, as CEO (Music Executive), manages Creators (role) and coordinates their occupations. Acts (role) represent organized performance groups or solo figures, with Promoter as one of their key occupations. The player balances Safe, Neutral, and Risky Alignments to navigate industry pressures and achieve Iconic Eras.`

## **`Vision Statement`**

`Record Label Simulator combines creativity, financial savvy, and systemic depth. Its ECS-driven design ensures scalability, modularity, and dynamic storytelling. The vision is to provide a simulation where art, commerce, and culture continuously shape one another.`

## **`Core Gameplay Loop (ECS)`**

1. `Direction → Era Management System`

2. `Creation → Content Creation System`

3. `Promotion → Promotion System`

4. `Legacy → Evaluation and Alignment Systems`

## **`Unique Selling Points`**

* `Strategic depth combining cultural nuance and systemic management.`

* `Dynamic trends driven by Audience Chunks, Critics, and social chatter.`

* `Flexible Era planning cycles where Themes, Moods, and Alignment interact.`

* `Scalable ECS architecture ensuring long-term replayability.`

## **`End Goals`**

`The player aims to secure chart dominance, deliver Iconic Eras, and achieve independence through robust infrastructure and cultural impact across Gaia.`
