`# Record Label Simulator - Player Scenario`

`## Player Role`  
`The **Player**, acting as the **Music Executive**, is responsible for managing all aspects of their **Record Label**.`

`## Gameplay Algorithm`  
`1. **Quests from CEO**:`  
   `- The **CEO** provides the Player with objectives and goals to steer the direction of the **Record Label**.`

`2. **Communities and Audience Interaction**:`  
   `- Identify **General Audience** preferences and potential **Guides** within **Communities**.`  
   `- Observe specific **Stans** within the audience for targeted engagement.`

`3. **Auditions for Creators**:`  
   `- Conduct auditions to determine which **Community Members** are interested in becoming **Creators**.`

`4. **Engagement on eyeriSocial**:`  
   `- Interact with **Independent Community Members** on the in-game social platform.`  
   `- Extend **Contracts** to **Community Members** to join the **Record Label**.`

`5. **Contract Negotiations**:`  
   `- Offer **Contracts** to potential **Creators** with an advance and a profit-sharing model.`

`6. **Act Formation and Management**:`  
   `- Assemble **Creators** into Acts.`  
   `- Post-contract conclusion, negotiate with **(deprecated term)** to continue, cancel, or adjust **Act** contracts (upgrades only).`

`7. **Making Decisions on Contract Upgrades**:`  
   `- If an **Act** requests an upgrade, the **Player** decides whether to accept.`  
   `- Declining an upgrade may result in increased **Catharsis**, influencing content creation.`

`8. **Collaboration and Content Creation**:`  
   `- Collaborate with other IDs or **Record Labels**.`  
   `- Start a new Era to commence content creation for an **Act**.`
