### **`Structure Leasing and Management in "Record Label Simulator"`**

**`Lease Details`**`:`

* **`Lease Duration`**`: Each structure has a 4-year lease paid upfront.`  
* **`Renewal Condition`**`: If the structure does not have enough funds to renew the lease for the upcoming 4 years upfront (for a total of up to 8 years), the structure is destroyed from the scene.`

### **`Example Scenario`**

1. **`Initial Lease`**`:`  
   * `A Record Label leases a recording studio from eyeriStructures for 4 years, paying the lease amount upfront.`  
2. **`Revenue Generation`**`:`  
   * `The studio is used for creating and finalizing tracks, generating revenue through physical and digital sales.`  
3. **`Lease Renewal`**`:`  
   * `At the end of the 4-year lease, the Record Label must have enough funds to renew the lease for another 4 years.`  
   * `If the Label does not have sufficient funds for the renewal, the structure is destroyed, and all associated IDs, modifiers, and content items are returned to the player's inventory with their percentage completion.`

### **`Financial Management`**

1. **`Lease Payment`**`:`  
   * **`Initial Lease`**`: Paid upfront for 4 years.`  
   * **`Renewal`**`: Must be paid upfront for the next 4 years at the end of the initial lease period.`  
2. **`Revenue Tracking`**`:`  
   * `Players need to ensure continuous revenue generation to accumulate sufficient funds for lease renewal.`
