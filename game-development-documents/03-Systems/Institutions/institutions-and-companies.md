# **`Record Label Simulator — Institutions and Companies`**

## **`Purpose`**

`Clarify which world entities are Institutions (world-owned, nonprofit) versus Companies (for-profit), how they interact with roles/occupations, and what gameplay systems they affect. Terminology matches the current canon: Acts, Creators, Audience Chunks, CCC, Critics Councils, Alignment (Safe/Neutral/Risky), Themes (5), and latest Moods (9).`

## **`Overview`**

`• Institutions: World-owned structures that provide public services, regulation, or civil infrastructure.`  
`• Companies: Private or corporate entities that seek profit via creation, distribution, or events.`  
`• Player: CEO (role) with the Music Executive occupation leading a Record Label (Company).`

## **`Institutions (World-Owned, Nonprofit)`**

### **`Creators Community Chamber (CCC)`**

`Function: Prepares Members and presents them as Creator IDs to Labels for signing. Replaces legacy A&R interview mechanics.`  
`Player Touchpoints: Browse Creator IDs, negotiate terms (Accept / Reconsider timer / Decline lock), finalize stage names.`  
`Systems: Signing, Alignment seeding, eyeriSocial announcements, timers & lockouts.`

### **`Critics Councils`**

`Function: Council-based rating bodies. Their evaluations modulate perceived Quality and chart velocity.`  
`Player Touchpoints: Monitor ratings; plan promotion and release windows accordingly.`  
`Systems: Alignment feedback, Trends/Charts, long-tail performance.`

### **`Residences`**

`Function: Housing for Members/Creators; influences stamina and availability.`  
`Player Touchpoints: Indirect—impacts Creator availability and schedule reliability.`  
`Systems: Stamina, Scheduling, Availability.`

### **`City Halls`**

`Function: Civic administration; Member spawning; permits and public notices.`  
`Player Touchpoints: Event permits, civic collaborations.`  
`Systems: Population, Events, Permits.`

### **`Central Banks (Macro)`**

`Function: Macro-economic parameters that influence regional costs and liquidity.`  
`Player Touchpoints: Indirect—affects leasing/ownership economics and campaign budgets.`  
`Systems: Economics, Pricing modifiers by nation.`

## **`Companies (For-Profit)`**

### **`Record Labels (Player Company)`**

`Function: Plan Eras, sign and support Creators, finance Projects/Tracks, run campaigns.`  
`Occupations: Music Executive (player), staff roles as needed.`  
`Systems: Era Planning (Direction, Creation, Promotion, Legacy), Budgeting, Contracts, Release Management.`

### **`Studios (Leasing & Ownership)`**

`Function: Spaces for writing, recording, mixing, mastering.`  
`Ownership: Lease (often via eyeriS Corp) or own outright.`  
`Systems: Efficiency, Maintenance, Upgrades, Quality contribution.`

### **`Venues`**

`Function: Live shows, listening parties, promotional events.`  
`Systems: Attendance, Engagement, Local Alignment shifts, Revenue.`

### **`Shopping Centers & Factories`**

`Function: Distribution and manufacturing for physical goods and merch.`  
`Member Occupations: Shopping Center Personnel (distribute/sell), Factory Personnel (manufacture/produce).`  
`Systems: Supply, Inventory, Revenue.`

### **`Broadcast Corporations`**

`Function: Television/radio/stream broadcasting; premieres and interviews.`  
`Member Occupation: Broadcast Personnel (present/show content).`  
`Systems: Reach, Public Perception, Momentum.`

### **`eyeriS Corp (Leasing & Services)`**

`Function: Provides studio leasing and related services across nations.`  
`Systems: Cross-national logistics, Cost baselines, Availability.`
