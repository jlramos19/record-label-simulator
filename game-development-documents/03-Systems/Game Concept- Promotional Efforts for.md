# **`Game Concept: Promotional Efforts for Content`**

This document outlines a game concept where promotional content is presented as mini-games. Players engage in these mini-games for 5 seconds in real life, translating to 2 hours of in-game time.

* 

`There will be mini-games for promotional content. The player will play a 5-second (in real life) minigame (2 hours in-game).`
