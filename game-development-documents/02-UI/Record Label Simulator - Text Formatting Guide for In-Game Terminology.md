**`Record Label Simulator — Text Formatting Guide for In-Game Terminology`**

---

## **`Role`**

`The individual or group that has a role and, where applicable, an OCCUPATION.`

**`Format:`** `Title Case Bold`

**`Examples:`**

* **`The Player`** `is the role.`  
* **`The Creator`** `is the role.`  
  ---

  ## **`OCCUPATION`**

`The occupation of a role determines the actions it can perform.`

**`Format:`** `ALL-CAPS BOLD`

**`Examples:`**

* `The MUSIC EXECUTIVE is the occupation of The Player.`  
* `The SONGWRITER, RECORDER, and/or PRODUCER are occupations of The Creator.`  
  ---

  ## **`Item`**

`An item created by a role.`

**`Format:`** `Title Case Italics`

**`Examples:`**

* *`Sheet Music`* `is created by a SONGWRITER Creator.`  
* *`Demo Recording`* `is created by a RECORDER Creator.`  
  ---

  ## **`PROPERTY`**

`A property of a role or item.`

**`Format:`** `ALL-CAPS ITALICS`

**`Examples:`**

* *`Sheet Music`* `can have a FREEDOM or LOYALTY THEME. A THEME is a property of items such as Sheet Music and Track.`  
* *`Demo Recording`* `can have a CHEERING or SADDENING MOOD. A MOOD is a property of items such as Demo Recording and Track.`  
  ---

  ## **`Area`**

`Where events happen.`

**`Format:`** `Title Case Bold + Italics`

**`Examples:`**

* ***`Recording Studio`*** `is where the SONGWRITER Creator creates Sheet Music.`  
* ***`Broadcast Studio`*** `is where the RECORDER Creator performs content.`  
  ---

  ## **`PURPOSE`**

`An area’s purpose determines what events can take place in it.`

**`Format:`** `ALL-CAPS BOLD + ITALICS`

---

## **`Modifier`**

`A modifier is a non-character effect that alters outcomes, costs, probabilities, timing, thresholds, or other values tied to roles, items, areas, actions, or events.`

**`Format:`** `Title Case Inline Code`

**`Examples:`**

* `Award Buzz increases engagement for a release window.`  
* `Schedule Pressure reduces completion reliability for time-sensitive work.`  
* `Critical Reception shifts the impact of a Track after release.`  
  ---

  ## **`action`**

`A verb that describes what roles can do.`

**`Format:`** `lowercase inline code`

**`Examples:`**

* `The Player signs creators.`  
* `A creator creates tracks and promotes content.`  
  ---

  ## **`EVENT`**

`The execution of a scheduled event.`

**`Format:`** `ALL-CAPS INLINE CODE`

**`Examples:`**

* `The RELEASE DATE of content is scheduled by The Player.`  
* `The ANNOUNCEMENT of content is scheduled by The Player.`  
  ---

  ## **`Programming`**

`The usual set of EVENTs of a designated entity.`

**`Format:`** `Title Case Inline Code`

**`Example:`**

* `Television Show broadcasts content provided by the Record Label from the Broadcast Studio to the Community’s Members for them to engage with and possibly consume via purchase and/or stream.`
