### **`Explanation of Terrain Features and Building Navigation`**

#### **`Terrain Features`**

1. **`Grid-Based Layout`**`:`  
   * `The game world is divided into a grid system, allowing for organized placement and navigation of structures and lots.`  
2. **`Top-Down View`**`:`  
   * `The game employs a top-down perspective, providing a clear and comprehensive view of the entire terrain and all its structures.`  
3. **`Building Lots`**`:`  
   * `Each lot on the terrain represents a building or structure.`  
   * `The lot displays the number of floors the building has, providing an immediate visual cue to the player.`  
4. **`Building Navigation`**`:`  
   * **`Click to Explore`**`: When a player clicks on a building lot, a panel appears.`  
   * **`Panel Display`**`: This panel shows each floor of the building in detail.`  
   * **`Floor Details`**`: Each floor can have up to 4 rooms, and these rooms are displayed within the panel.`  
   * **`Interactive Floors`**`: Players can interact with the floors and rooms through the panel, allowing for detailed exploration and management.`
