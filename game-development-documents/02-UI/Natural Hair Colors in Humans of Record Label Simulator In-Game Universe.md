# **`Natural Hair Colors in Humans of Record Label Simulator In-Game Universe`**

1. `Eumelanin-based colors:`  
   * `Black`  
     * `Jet Black: This is the darkest possible hair color and is common in people of various ethnic backgrounds.`  
     * `Blue Black: Some people have black hair with a blue undertone, giving it a slightly blue sheen in the light.`  
   * `Brown`  
     * `Dark Brown: This is a deeply pigmented dark cool shade of brown.`  
     * `Medium Brown: A fairly neutral brown hair color.`  
     * `Light Brown: Not quite dark, not quite blonde â€“ this color often features golden tones.`  
     * `Ash Brown: This brown shade incorporates cooler (grey) undertones.`  
2. `Pheomelanin-based colors:`  
   * `Ginger: Quite rare, ginger hair color is typically a light, warm occurrence of red.`  
   * `Red`  
     * `Bright Red: This hair color pops out due to its vibrant, noticeable hue.`  
     * `Deep Red: This color is darker with a more muted, burgundy coloration.`  
     * `Auburn: As mentioned previously, auburn is a brownish-red color, more muted than bright red.`  
   * `Blonde`  
     * `Platinum Blonde: Also known as "towheaded", this is the lightest shade of blonde.`  
     * `Ash Blonde: This is a light blonde with a grayish tint to it.`  
     * `Strawberry Blonde: Strawberry blonde is a blend of light red with a blonde tinge.`  
3. `Mixed eumelanin and pheomelanin-based colors:`  
   * `Blonde`  
     * `Golden Blonde: This color is a deeper, richer shade of blonde with a warm undertone.`  
     * `Honey Blonde: Honey blonde hair can be described as a medium golden shade with a hint of brown.`  
4. `Age-related hair colors:`  
   * `Grey: The color that hair tends to turn as people age due to a decrease in pigment.`  
   * `White: Hair that has lost all its pigment will turn white.`
