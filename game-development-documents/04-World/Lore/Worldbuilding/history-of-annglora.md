# **`Annglora`**

* `A cataclysmic event wiped out previous civilizations, leading to the establishment of Annglora around ~2025.`  
* `Initial population: 100 million`  
* `Located on the continent of Annglora`  
* `Government type: Commonwealth of Annglora`  
* `Inspiration: North America and Europe, reflecting characteristics commonly associated with the United States.`  
* `Known for environmental sustainability and advanced technology.`  
* `Strong influence on urban development, societal structuring, and cultural identity.`

# **`Record Labels:`**

* **`Annglora Record Label (ARL1): First-ever Record Label.`**  
* **`International Record Label (ARL2):`** `Focuses on global outreach and international collaborations.`  
* **`Hedren Record Label (ARL3):`** `Player-managed Label, where the player acts as a music executive.`

# **`Sensory Sage:`**

* **`Annglora`** `legally harvests and possibly uses Sensory Sage, a substance known for enhancing creativity; it is illegal in Crowlya but used covertly by some Labels there.`
