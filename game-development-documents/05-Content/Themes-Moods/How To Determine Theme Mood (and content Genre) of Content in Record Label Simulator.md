# **`How To Determine Theme & Mood (and content Genre) of Content in Record Label Simulator`**

## *`The five themes and nine moods for Record Label Simulator:`*

### **`Themes (5):`**

1. **`Freedom`** `– Represents individuality, liberation, or breaking free (e.g., We Can't Stop by Miley Cyrus).`  
2. **`Loyalty`** `– Focuses on dedication, devotion, or love (e.g., All I Ask by Adele).`  
3. **`Ambition`** `– Centers on striving for goals, desires, or aspirations (e.g., Change Your Mind (No Seas Cortes) by Britney Spears).`  
4. **`Morality`** `– Explores ethics, justice, or right versus wrong (e.g., The Man by Taylor Swift).`  
5. **`Power`** `– Conveys strength, dominance, or flaunting superiority (e.g., BO$$ by Fifth Harmony).`

### **`Moods (9):`**

1. **`Cheering`**  
2. **`Saddening`**  
3. **`Thrilling`**  
4. **`Angering`**  
5. **`Calming`**  
6. **`Energizing`**  
7. **`Uplifting`**  
8. **`Boring`**  
9. **`Daring`**

`Each track’s theme is determined by its lyrics and context, while the mood is informed by the vocal delivery and instrumental tone. These combine to define the in-game content genre in the format: "theme but it's mood" (e.g., freedom but it's calming).`

`IMPORTANT NOTE: names of in-game content genres are stylized in all lowercase.`
