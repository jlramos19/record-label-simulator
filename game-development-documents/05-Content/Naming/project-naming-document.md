### **`Project Naming Document`**

`This document outlines the naming conventions for the web application and the game itself, ensuring clarity and consistency across all stages of development and release.`

---

### **`1. Working Name: Content Company Simulator`**

* **`Purpose`**`: The working name "Content Company Simulator" serves as the internal name of the web app during development.`  
* **`Reasoning`**`:`  
  * `Provides a broad focus during the early stages of development.`  
  * `Emphasizes the simulation of content creation and management processes.`  
  * `Offers flexibility for exploring diverse mechanics and gameplay systems without restricting scope.`  
* **`Application Context`**`:`  
  * `The repo, file names, and development tooling use Content Company Simulator as the primary internal identifier.`

---

### **`2. Actual Game Name: Record Label Simulator`**

* **`Purpose`**`: The actual name of the game is Record Label Simulator.`  
* **`Reasoning`**`:`  
  * `Reflects the game's refined focus on simulating the music industry.`  
  * `Highlights the core gameplay loop of managing Record Labels, creators, and content production.`  
  * `Resonates with the target audience by clearly communicating the game’s niche.`  
* **`Key Elements of the Name`**`:`  
  * **`Music`**`: Specifies the industry focus.`  
  * `Label: Emphasizes relationships, collaborations, and strategic management.`  
  * **`Simulator`**`: Identifies the game as a simulation experience.`

---

### **`3. Summary of Usage`**

| `Aspect` | `Name` |
| ----- | ----- |
| `Web App Internal Name` | `Content Company Simulator` |
| `Game Release Name` | `Record Label Simulator` |
| `Development Files and Tooling` | `Content Company Simulator` |
| `Marketing, Branding, and Launch` | `Record Label Simulator` |

---

### **`4. Rationale for Dual Naming`**

* **`Content Company Simulator`** `as the working name provides flexibility during development and aligns with the broader theme of content management systems.`  
* **`Record Label Simulator`** `as the actual game name narrows the scope, enhances marketability, and effectively communicates the game’s focus on the music industry.`

`This dual naming approach ensures clarity across development, branding, and player-facing materials while maintaining consistency and focus as the project evolves.`
