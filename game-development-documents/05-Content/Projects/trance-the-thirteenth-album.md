`### TRANCE - THE THIRTEENTH ALBUM`

`- "Who Are You?": Explores the shock and surprise caused by the actions of the muse.`  
`- "I'm Warning You": The writer warns the muse about their own dangerous nature.`  
`- "My Soulmate": The muse claims the writer is their soulmate, from the writer's perspective.`  
`- "When You Come": Focuses on the anticipation and expectations surrounding a trip the muse will make to visit the writer, despite obstacles.`  
`- "No, You Fucked Up": Written from the writer's perspective, this track explores the blame and conflict between the writer and the muse after an argument.`  
`- "The Dry Well": The writer reflects on the muse's lack of initiative in important aspects of life and moving forward.`  
`- "The Catalyst": The writer reflects on the muse's most hurtful indirect action toward them.`  
`- "The Paradise You Promised": The writer ponders with disillusioned realization that what once seemed like the dream life is now something they no longer desire.`
