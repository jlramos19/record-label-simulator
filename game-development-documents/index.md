# Record Label Simulator - GDD Index

This folder mirrors Drive canon and organizes the GDD into functional lanes. Use the subfolders first; `99-Archive` holds unsorted/legacy notes.

## Folder Map
- `01-Canon` - Core GDD, overview, governance, glossary, and MVP direction.
- `02-UI` - UI/UX design system, visual rules, color systems, and interface notes.
- `03-Systems` - Simulation rules (time, charts, population, economy, quests, rollout, pipelines).
  - `03-Systems/Audience` - Audience mechanics and chunk properties.
  - `03-Systems/Economy` - Economy specs and models (see `03-Systems/Economy/Index.md`).
    - `03-Systems/Economy/Currency` - Currency systems and country acronyms.
    - `03-Systems/Economy/Pricing` - Track/project/format pricing tables and pricing rules.
    - `03-Systems/Economy/Revenue` - Revenue streams, splits, and broadcasting rules.
    - `03-Systems/Economy/Costs` - Leasing, maintenance, and operating costs.
    - `03-Systems/Economy/Loans` - Loan and interest rate rules.
    - `03-Systems/Economy/Transactions` - Currency flow and organization transaction logic.
    - `03-Systems/Economy/Country-Models` - Country-specific transaction models.
  - `03-Systems/Infrastructure` - Structures, leasing, studios, and facilities.
  - `03-Systems/Institutions` - Institutions, corporations, and governance structures.
- `04-World` - Gaia world data (nations, geography, economics) plus a dedicated Lore lane.
  - `04-World/Lore` - Narrative canon, character lore, dialogue, names, and lyrics.
- `05-Content` - Content mechanics: genres, themes, moods, projects, tracklists, naming, and creation guides.
  - `05-Content/Genres` - Content genres and inspiration sets.
  - `05-Content/Themes-Moods` - Theme/mood systems, weights, and instrumentation.
  - `05-Content/Projects` - Project-level examples and proposals.
  - `05-Content/Tracklists` - Tracklist structures and lists.
  - `05-Content/Tracks` - Track concepts (non-lyric).
  - `05-Content/Naming` - Naming conventions and versioning.
  - `05-Content/Guides` - Content creation and pipeline guidance.
- `06-Characters` - Creators, acts, NPCs, roles, and dialogue.
- `07-Engineering` - Technical/implementation notes (web app, TypeScript, architecture, tooling).
- `08-Reference` - Templates, prompts, and formatting references.
- `99-Archive` - Legacy or uncategorized notes pending review.
