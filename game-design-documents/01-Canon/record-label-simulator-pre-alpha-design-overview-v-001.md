# **`# Record Label Simulator - Pre-Alpha Design Overview (v. 0.0.1)`**

The **Player** as the **Music Executive** `signs` **Creators** and `hires` **Guides**.

The **Creator** is a **Role** that can have three ~~PROFESSIONS~~: **Songwriter**, **Vocalist**, and **Producer**.

The **Creator** with a **Songwriter** ~~PROFESSION~~ can `create` *Sheet Music* with one of five ~~THEMES~~: \~FREEDOM.  
The **Creator** with a **Vocalist** ~~PROFESSION~~ can `create` *Demo Recording*.  
The **Creator** with a **Producer** ~~PROFESSION~~ can `create` *Track*.
