\#\#\# Sheet Music in Record Label Simulator

\*\*Title\*\*: Sheet Music

\*\*Short Description of Function\*\*:  
\- Sheet music establishes the lyrical theme for a song, setting the direction for its lyrical content.

\*\*How to Get Item\*\*:  
\- created by placing a songwriter creator ID in a Recording Studio Structure’s ID slot. The theme assigned is influenced by the songwriter’s two preferred themes.

\*\*What to Do with Item\*\*:  
\- Players use sheet music to create a demo recording by placing a vocalist creator ID in a recording studio structure’s ID slot. This demo recording then determines the mood of the song. Notably, the same piece of sheet music can be used to produce multiple demo recordings in different moods, reflecting various interpretations of the theme.

\*\*Is Item Consumable\*\*:  
\- No, sheet music is not consumed after being turned into demos. It can be reused to explore different musical interpretations of the same thematic content.

\*\*Etc.\*\*:  
\- The game features five lyrical themes: FREEDOM, LOYALTY, AMBITION, MORALITY, and POWER. Each piece of sheet music is associated with one of these themes, based on the songwriter's preferences. Additionally, the quality of the sheet music is determined by a range that reflects the songwriter creator’s skill level. Songs derived from the sheet music can be recorded in various moods, such as calm, uplifting, sad, energetic, exciting, angry, happy, or dark, offering a wide range of emotional expressions for the game's music.
