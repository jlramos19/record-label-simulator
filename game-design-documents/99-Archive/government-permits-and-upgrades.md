Global Expansion Permits: Special permits that enable players to enter new fictional countries or markets.  
Strategic Choices: Players must strategically decide which permits to pursue, considering their current goals and the evolving needs of the label.

Challenges and Quests: Quests and challenges related to government permits can create engaging gameplay objectives, driving players to explore different aspects of the game.

Government Relations: Building and maintaining good relationships with virtual government bodies can increase the availability of permits.
