`### Game Concept: Organizations in Record Label Simulator`

`- **Organizations**: Entities within the game that manage or influence various aspects of the music industry.`  
  `- **Corporations**: For-profit organizations involved in commercial activities.`  
    `- **Record Labels**: Corporations focused on managing and producing musical content.`  
    `- **Broadcast Labels**: Corporations specializing in distributing and broadcasting media content.`  
    `- **Factories**: Facilities for manufacturing music-related products or content.`  
    `- **Shopping Centers**: Retail spaces where items and content can be bought or sold.`  
    `- **EyeriS Corporation**: The mega-corporation that leases all studios to Record Labels, simplifying in-game studio management.`

  `- **Institutions**: Nonprofit organizations focused on non-commercial, supportive, or educational roles.`  
    `- **C.R.A.F.T.**: **Creative Research and Advancement of Future Talent** - Studios dedicated to improving the skills of members who aspire to become creators rather than personnel in factories or shopping centers.`
