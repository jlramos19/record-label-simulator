`### Stamina Mechanics for Occupations in "Record Label Simulator"`

`#### Overview`  
`This document summarizes the three main occupations (Songwriters, Recorders, Producers), detailing the content they create, the ticks required, the in-game and real-life time equivalents, and the stamina consumption.`

`### Songwriters:`  
`- **Occupation**: Songwriter`  
`- **Content Created**: Sheet Music`  
`- **Ticks per Creation**: 25,000,000 ticks`  
`- **In-Game Time**: 1 hour`  
`- **Real-Life Time**: 2.5 seconds`  
`- **Stamina Type**: Mental`  
`- **Stamina per Creation**: 25 stamina units`

`### Recorders:`  
`- **Occupation**: Recorder`  
`- **Content Created**: Demo Recordings`  
`- **Ticks per Creation**: 50,000,000 ticks`  
`- **In-Game Time**: 2 hours`  
`- **Real-Life Time**: 5 seconds`  
`- **Stamina Type**: Physical`  
`- **Stamina per Creation**: 50 stamina units`

`### Producers:`  
`- **Occupation**: Producer`  
`- **Content Created**: Finalized Tracks`  
`- **Ticks per Creation**: 75,000,000 ticks`  
`- **In-Game Time**: 3 hours`  
`- **Real-Life Time**: 7.5 seconds`  
`- **Stamina Types**: Both Physical and Mental`  
`- **Stamina per Creation**: 150 stamina units (75 each for physical and mental)`

`### Summary of Stamina Usage:`  
`1. **Songwriters**:`  
   `- Create Sheet Music.`  
   `- **25,000,000 ticks** per sheet.`  
   `- 1 hour in-game, 2.5 seconds real-life.`  
   `- **25 mental stamina units** per sheet.`  
   `- **400 stamina** allows creation of **16 sheets**.`

`2. **Recorders**:`  
   `- Create Demo Recordings.`  
   `- **50,000,000 ticks** per demo.`  
   `- 2 hours in-game, 5 seconds real-life.`  
   `- **50 physical stamina units** per demo.`  
   `- **400 stamina** allows creation of **8 demos**.`

`3. **Producers**:`  
   `- Finalize Tracks.`  
   `- **75,000,000 ticks** per track.`  
   `- 3 hours in-game, 7.5 seconds real-life.`  
   `- **150 stamina units** (75 physical + 75 mental) per track.`  
   `- **400 stamina** allows creation of **2 tracks**.`

`This comprehensive summary provides a clear understanding of the roles, content creation processes, time requirements, and stamina usage for the three main occupations in "Record Label Simulator."`
