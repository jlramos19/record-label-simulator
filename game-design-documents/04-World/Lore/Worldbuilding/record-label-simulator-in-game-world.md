# **`Record Label Simulator In-Game World Structure`**

## **`Global In-Game World Hierarchy`**

* `Countries: Compose the largest geographic classification.`  
* `States: Located within Countries.`  
* `Cities: Located within States.`  
* `Neighborhoods: Located within Cities.`  
* `Areas: Specific lots located within Neighborhoods.`

### **`Area (Lot) Specifications`**

#### **`Lot Sizes and Building Capacities`**

**`Capital Cities:`**

**`Small lots:`**

* **`Lot Dimensions`**`: 25m x 25m`  
* **`Area`**`: 625 m²`  
* **`Perimeter`**`: 100 meters`  
* **`Building Capacity`**`: Can support buildings with up to 40 floors`  
* **`Floor Height`**`: 3 meters per floor`  
* **`Max Building Height`**`: 120 meters (40 floors × 3 meters per floor)`

**`Large lots:`**

* **`Lot Dimensions`**`: 25m x 50m`  
* **`Area`**`: 1,250 m²`  
* **`Perimeter`**`: 150 meters`  
* **`Building Capacity`**`: Can support buildings with up to 80 floors`  
* **`Floor Height`**`: 3 meters per floor`  
* **`Max Building Height`**`: 240 meters (80 floors × 3 meters per floor)` 

Community Member Height
