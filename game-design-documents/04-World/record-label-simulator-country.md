## **`Annglora`**

* `Demonym: Anngloran`

* `Continent Influence: North + South America (Native, environmental, spiritual heritage)`

* `Primary Color: Green`

* `Secondary Color: Theme/mood-driven (darker for higher Morality)`

* `Clothing Style: Flowing, tribal-inspired fashion (sometimes sexy variants)`

* `Hair Length Tendency: Longer`

* `Population: Large`

* `Religion: Strongly religious`

* `Politics: Liberal`

* `Geography: Mostly northern; capital (Bloomville) in the south near subtropical forest`

* `Economy: Strong currency, low cost of living, abundant natural resources`

* `Cultural Identity: Environmental preservation, spirituality, devotion`

* `Supernatural Beliefs: Medium emphasis on magic/supernatural`

* `Social Stance (LGBTQ+): Legal and accepting`

* `Relations: Defends with Crowlya; threatened by Bytenza`

---

## **`Bytenza`**

* `Demonym: Bytenese`

* `Continent Influence: Asia + Middle East (techno-political power, urban concentration)`

* `Primary Color: Cyan`

* `Secondary Color: Theme/mood-driven (darker for higher Power)`

* `Clothing Style: Tech and business attire, casual, retro hybrids`

* `Hair Length Tendency: Mid-length`

* `Population: Medium`

* `Religion: Nonreligious`

* `Politics: Moderate`

* `Geography: Far eastern region, mostly northern territory`

* `Economy: Balanced currency, moderate cost of living, expertise in law/politics/economy`

* `Cultural Identity: Technological drive, cultural mosaic, focus on governance and power`

* `Supernatural Beliefs: Low emphasis`

* `Social Stance (LGBTQ+): Conservative`

* `Relations: Threatens Annglora; maintains balanced ties with Crowlya`

---

## **`Crowlya`**

* `Demonym: Crowlish`

* `Continent Influence: Africa + Caribbean (regal aesthetics, diasporic cultural blends)`

* `Primary Color: Gold`

* `Secondary Color: Theme/mood-driven (darker for higher Ambition)`

* `Clothing Style: Regal, elegant, and distinguished`

* `Hair Length Tendency: Shorter`

* `Population: Small`

* `Religion: Moderately religious`

* `Politics: Conservative`

* `Geography: Central region, mostly southern; archipelagic with main island and smaller surrounding isles`

* `Economy: Weak currency, high cost of living, tech-based and conservative-leaning`

* `Cultural Identity: Blend of technological advancement and Caribbean-inspired regality`

* `Supernatural Beliefs: High emphasis on destiny, zodiac, and supernatural forces`

* `Social Stance (LGBTQ+): Mixed tolerance, often masked representation`

* `Relations: Defends Annglora, balanced with Bytenza`
