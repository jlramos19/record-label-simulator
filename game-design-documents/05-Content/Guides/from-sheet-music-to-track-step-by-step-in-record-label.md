`### From Sheet Music to Track: Step-by-Step`

`### Create Sheet Music`

`**Select Recording Studio Structure**:`  
   `- **Place Items in Slots**:`   
     `- **ID Slot**: Place the **Songwriter Creator ID**.`  
     `- **Content Slot**: Greyed out for Songwriters.`  
     `- **Modifier Slot**: Place any desired *modifier* (*Sensory Sage* or *Dispatch Dust*).`

`**Output**:`  
   `- *Sheet Music*: Created and added to the player's inventory.`

`### Create Demo Recording`

`**Select Recording Studio Structure**:`  
   `- **Place Items in Slots**:`   
     `- **ID Slot**: Place the **Vocalist Creator ID**.`  
     `- **Content Slot**: Place the *Sheet Music*.`  
     `- **Modifier Slot**: Place any desired *modifier* (*Sensory Sage* or *Dispatch Dust*).`

`**Output**:`  
   `- *Demo Recording*: Created and added to the player's inventory.`

`### Create Track`

`**Select Recording Studio Structure**:`  
   `- **Place Items in Slots**:`   
     `- **ID Slot**: Place the **Producer Creator ID**.`  
     `- **Content Slot**: Place the *Demo Recording*.`  
     `- **Modifier Slot**: Place any desired *modifier* (*Sensory Sage* or *Dispatch Dust*).`

`**Output**:`  
   `- *Track*: Created and added to the player's inventory.`

`### Schedule Track`

`**Select Calendar**:`  
   `- **Place Items in Slots**:`  
     `- **Content Slot**: Place the *Track* in the Content Slot in the Calendar.`  
   `- **Available Slots**: The calendar will show slots for content to be scheduled hourly. For releasing *Tracks* and *Projects*, only the "FRI - 12AM" slots are available.`  
   `- **Scheduling Requirements**:`  
     `- **Digital Tracks**: Schedule at least 1 week in advance.`  
     `- **Physical Tracks & Digital Projects**: Schedule at least 2 weeks in advance.`  
     `- **Physical Projects**: Schedule at least 4 weeks in advance.`

`### Promote Track`

`To promote content, the **Act ID** must be placed in a ***Broadcasting Studio*** that has a slot available for content to be promoted. The slot determines if the promotion will be a live performance or interview, depending on the type of program.`

`- **Process**:`  
  `- **Place Items in Slots**:`  
    `- **ID Slot**: Place the **Act ID** in the ***Broadcasting Studio***.`  
    `- **Content Slot**: Available for the promotion type (live performance or interview).`  
  `- **Broadcast**: Once the **Act ID** promotes content in this way, it is broadcasted to **Members**.`

`- **Outcome**:`  
  `- **Awareness**: **Members** are made aware of the *Track* or *Project*.`  
  `- **Engagement**: **Members** will then have a possibility of buying or streaming the content based on their preferences and availability.`
