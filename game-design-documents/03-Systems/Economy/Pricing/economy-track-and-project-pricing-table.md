## **`Economy - Track and Project Pricing (Web MVP)`**

### **`Baseline Prices`**

* **`Digital single:`** `$0.69`  
* **`Physical single:`** `$4.99`  
* **`Physical release fee:`** `$500 per format`  
* **`Streaming subscription (eyeriSound):`** `$14.99/month`  

### **`Project Type Limits`**

* **`Single:`** `1-4 tracks`  
* **`EP:`** `5-7 tracks`  
* **`Album:`** `8-32 tracks`  

### **`Physical Multipliers (Unit Price)`**

* **`Single:`** `1.00x`  
* **`EP:`** `1.55x`  
* **`Album:`** `2.25x`  

### **`Notes`**

* `The detailed per-track pricing tables and the 30-70 pricing pattern are legacy design targets and are not active in the web MVP.`
* `Physical run estimates use the baseline single price plus the multipliers above to compute unit price and cost.`
