### **`Economy - Pricing and Track Limits by Format (MVP)`**

#### **`Track Limits (Canonical)`**

* `Single Project: 1-4 tracks`
* `EP Project: 5-7 tracks`
* `Album Project: 8-32 tracks`

#### **`Pricing Baselines (Web MVP)`**

* `Digital single base price: $0.69`
* `Physical single base price: $4.99`
* `Physical release fee: $500 per format`
* `Physical unit cost = max($0.50, unit price * 0.35)`

#### **`Physical Unit Price Multipliers`**

* `Single: 1.00x`
* `EP: 1.55x`
* `Album: 2.25x`

#### **`Notes`**

* `The detailed 30-70 per-track pricing table is a legacy design target and is not active in the web MVP.`
* `Physical run recommendations use the unit price multipliers above to estimate unit price and cost.`
