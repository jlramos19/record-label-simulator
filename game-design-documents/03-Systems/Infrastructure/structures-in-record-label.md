**`Structures in Record Label Simulator`**

1. **`Studios`**`: Output Content items.`  
2. **`Venues`**`: Promote Content and output Content depending on what IDs are placed and what Organization owns them.`  
3. **`Offices`**`: Where the player "works" and where the Record Label operates from, and where community members work to be able to buy and stream content.`  
4. **`Residences`**`: Where members reside and consume content.`  
5. **`Factories`**`: Output content to content shopping centers.`  
6. **`Content Shopping Centers`**`: Where members buy content from.`  
7. **`City Halls`**`: Output the new or immigrating members of the city.`  
8. **`Central Banks`**`: Control the currency flow of the city and it is a commodity currency tied to content creation.`  
9. **`Creators Community Chambers`**`: Where members who aspire to be creators go to be signed by Record Labels.`  
10. **`Critics Councils`**`: Rate the quality of content which influences its consumption rate.`

`·  Studios:`

* **`Recording Studios`**`: Used by Record Labels for creating main content (projects and tracks).`  
* **`TV Shows`**`: Used by broadcast corporations to produce TV shows and other televised content.`

`·  Offices:`

**`Critics Councils`**`: There are five councils, each evaluating and rating the quality of content based on its Alignment (how it evokes reactions in members) and age appropriateness:`

* **`Safe Council`**`: Rates content with a bias towards being family-friendly and suitable for all ages.`  
  * **`Neutral-Safe Council`**`: Rates content with a slight bias towards safe content, suitable for ages 8 and up.`  
  * **`Neutral Council`**`: Provides balanced and unbiased ratings, suitable for ages 12 and up.`  
  * **`Neutral-Risky Council`**`: Rates content with a slight bias towards riskier themes, suitable for ages 16 and up.`  
  * **`Risky Council`**`: Rates content with a bias towards mature or explicit themes, suitable for ages 20 and up.`  
* **`Content Shopping Centers`**`: There are 135 shopping centers with 4-year leases that expire and are then rebuilt to reflect the supply and demand of the content genre and Alignment of content. An example would be: Risky Uplifting Mood Shopping Center.`

  ### **`Content Rating System`**

`Each piece of content is rated by all five Critics Councils, and their ratings are influenced by their biases. Here's an example of how a piece of content with a true quality of 80 would be rated:`

* **`Safe Council`**`: 100`  
* **`Neutral-Safe Council`**`: 90`  
* **`Neutral Council`**`: 80 (true quality)`  
* **`Neutral-Risky Council`**`: 70`  
* **`Risky Council`**`: 60`

`The true quality is debuffed or buffed by the bias of each council, providing a range of ratings that reflect different audience perspectives.`
