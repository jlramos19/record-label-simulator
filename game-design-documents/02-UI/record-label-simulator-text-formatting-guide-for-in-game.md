\# Record Label Simulator \- Text Formatting Guide for In-Game Terminology  
—

\#\# \*\*Character Role\*\*:  
The Individual or Group that has a \*\*Role\*\* and, where applicable, an \*\*OCCUPATION\*\*.

The \*\*Character Role\*\* is formatted as \*\*Title Case Bold\*\*.

Examples:

\- The \*\*Player\*\* is the \*\*Role\*\*.  
\- The \*\*Creator\*\* is the \*\*Role\*\*.

\---  
\#\# \*\*CHARACTER OCCUPATION\*\*:  
The \*\*OCCUPATION\*\* of a \*\*Character\*\* determines the \`actions\` it can perform.

The \*\*CHARACTER OCCUPATION\*\* is formatted as \*\*ALL-CAPS BOLD\*\*.

Examples:  
\- The \*\*MUSIC EXECUTIVE\*\* is the \*\*OCCUPATION\*\* of the \*\*Player\*\*.  
\- The \*\*SONGWRITER\*\*, \*\*RECORDER\*\*, and/or \*\*PRODUCER\*\* \*\*OCCUPATIONS\*\* of the \*\*Creator\*\*.

\---  
\#\# \*\*\*ORGANIZATION\*\*\*:  
An \*\*\*area\*\*\*'s \*\*\*ORGANIZATION\*\*\* determines what events can take place in it.

An \*\*\*area\*\*\*'s \*\*\*ORGANIZATION\*\*\* is formatted as \*\*\*ALL-CAPS BOLD AND ITALICS\*\*\*.

Examples:  
\- A \*\*\*Record Label\*\*\* \*\*\*Building\*\*\* is where a \*\*MUSIC EXECUTIVE\*\* \`manages\` \*\*Creators\*\*.  
\- An \*\*\*ORGANIZATION\*\*\* \*\*\*Building\*\*\* is where a \*\*Critic\*\* \`rates\` \*Content\* to determine its \*QUALITY\*.

\---  
\#\# \*\*\*Structure\*\*\*:  
A building or venue that physically represents an \*\*\*ORGANIZATION\*\*\*.

A \*\*\*Structure\*\*\* is formatted as \*\*\*Title Case Bold and Italics\*\*\*.

Examples:  
\- A \*\*\*RECORDING\*\*\* \*\*\*Studio\*\*\* is where the \*\*SONGWRITER\*\* \*\*Creator\*\* \`creates\` \*Sheet Music\*.  
\- A \*\*\*BROADCAST\*\*\* \*\*\*Studio\*\*\* is where the \*\*RECORDER\*\* \*\*Creator\*\* \`performs\` \*Content\*.

\---  
\#\# \*\*\*area\*\*\*:  
An \*\*\*area\*\*\* is the terrain where \*\*\*Structures\*\*\* are placed.

An \*\*\*area\*\*\* is formatted as \*\*\*lowercase bold and italics\*\*\*.

Examples:  
\- A \*\*\*RECORDING\*\*\* \*\*\*Studio\*\*\* \*\*\*area\*\*\* is where the \*\*SONGWRITER\*\* \*\*Creator\*\* \`creates\` \*Sheet Music\*.  
\- A \*\*\*BROADCAST\*\*\* \*\*\*Studio\*\*\* \*\*\*area\*\*\* is where the \*\*RECORDER\*\* \*\*Creator\*\* \`performs\` \*Content\*.

\---  
\#\# \*Item\*:  
An \*Item\* \`created\` by a \*\*Character\*\*.

The \*Item\* is formatted as \*Title Case Italics\*.

Examples:  
\- A \*Sheet Music\* is \`created\` by a \*\*SONGWRITER\*\* \*\*Creator\*\*.  
\- A \*Demo Recording\* is \`created\` by a \*\*RECORDER\*\* \*\*Creator\*\*.

\---  
\#\# \*PROPERTY\*:  
The \*PROPERTY\* of a \*\*Character\*\* or \*Item\*.

The \*PROPERTY\* is formatted as \*ALL-CAPS ITALICS\*.

Examples:  
\- A \*Sheet Music\* can have a \*FREEDOM\* or \*LOYALTY\* \*THEME\*. A \*THEME\* is a \*PROPERTY\* of \*Items\* such as \*Sheet Music\* and \*Track\*.  
\- A \*Demo Recording\* can have a \*CHEERING\* or \*SADDENING\* \*MOOD\*. A \*MOOD\* is a \*PROPERTY\* of \*Items\* such as \*Demo Recording\* and \*Track\*.

\---  
\#\# \*modifier\*:  
A \*modifier\* of a \*\*\*Structure\*\*\*’s output.

A \*modifier\* is formatted as \*lowercase italics\*.

Examples:  
\- A \*sensory sage\* can make a \*\*\*Structure\*\*\* output \*Content\* with higher \*QUALITY\* at a slower \*SPEED\*.  
\- A \*dispatch dust\* can make a \*\*\*Structure\*\*\* output \*Content\* with lower \*QUALITY\* at a faster \*SPEED\*.

\---  
\#\# \`action\`:  
An \`action\` is a verb that describes what \*\*Characters\*\* can do.

An \`action\` is formatted as \`lowercase inline code\`.

Examples:  
\- The \*\*Player\*\* as the \*\*Music Executive\*\* \`signs\` \*\*Creators\*\*.  
\- A \*\*Creator\*\* \`creates\` \*Tracks\* and \`promotes\` \*Content\*.

\---  
\#\# \`EVENT\`:  
An \`EVENT\` is the execution of a scheduled event.

An \`EVENT\` is formatted as \`ALL-CAPS INLINE CODE\`.

Examples:  
\- The \`RELEASE DATE\` of \*Content\* is \`scheduled\` by the \*\*Music Executive\*\*.  
\- The \`ANNOUNCEMENT\` of \*Content\* is \`announced\` by the \*\*Music Executive\*\*.

\---  
\#\# \`Programming\`:  
A \`Programming\` is the usual set of \`EVENTS\` of a designated entity.

A \`Programming\` is formatted as \`Title Case Inline Code\`.

Examples:  
\- The \`Television Show\` \`broadcasts\` \*Content\* provided by the \*\*\*MUSIC\*\*\* \*\*\*Label\*\*\* from the \*\*\*BROADCAST\*\*\* \*\*\*Studio\*\*\* to the \*\*Community\*\*'s \*\*MEMBERS\*\* for them to \`engage\` with and possibly \`consume\` in the form of a \`purchase\` and/or \`stream\`, which are \`actions\` that \*\*Community\*\* \*\*MEMBERS\*\* can perform.

—  
Here is the comprehensive list of the formatting styles for "Record Label Simulator":

1\. \*\*Character Role\*\*: \*\*Title Case Bold\*\*  
2\. \*\*CHARACTER OCCUPATION\*\*: \*\*ALL-CAPS BOLD\*\*  
3\. \*\*\*ORGANIZATION\*\*\*: \*\*\*ALL-CAPS BOLD AND ITALICS\*\*\*  
4\. \*\*\*Structure\*\*\*: \*\*\*Title Case Bold and Italics\*\*\*  
5\. \*\*\*area\*\*\*: \*\*\*lowercase bold and italics\*\*\*  
6\. \*Item\*: \*Title Case Italics\*  
7\. \*PROPERTY\*: \*ALL-CAPS ITALICS\*  
8\. \*modifier\*: \*lowercase italics\*  
9\. \`Programming\`: \`Title Case Inline Code\`  
10\. \`EVENT\`: \`ALL-CAPS INLINE CODE\`  
11\. \`action\`: \`lowercase inline code\`

This list now reflects all the defined text formats for different terminologies within your game.
