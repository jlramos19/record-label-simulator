`### Time and Ticks for Content Creation in Record Label Simulator`

`#### Overview`  
`This document outlines the time required (both in real life and in-game) and the number of ticks needed to complete the creation of sheet music, demo recordings, and tracks. It also specifies the necessary IDs and structures involved in the process.`

`#### Sheet Music Creation`

`- **ID Required**: Songwriter Creator ID`  
`- **Structure**: Recording Studio`  
`- **Real-Life Time**: 1 second`  
`- **In-Game Time**: 1 day`  
`- **Ticks**: 25,000,000 ticks`  
`- **Stamina**: 25`  
`#### Demo Recording Creation`

`- **ID Required**: Recorder Creator ID`  
`- **Structure**: Recording Studio`  
`- **Real-Life Time**: 5 seconds`  
`- **In-Game Time**: 2 hours`  
`- **Ticks**: 50,000,000 ticks`  
`- **Stamina**: 50`

`#### Track Finalization`

`- **ID Required**: Producer (Player) ID`  
`- **Structure**: Recording Studio`  
`- **Real-Life Time**: 7.5 seconds`  
`- **In-Game Time**: 3 hours`  
`- **Ticks**: 75,000,000 ticks`  
`- **Stamina**: 150 (75 x2 since stamina is divided into 200 mental and 200 physical for a total of 400 recharged each 8 in-game hours.)`

`#### Summary`

`- **Sheet Music**: **2.5 seconds (real-life)**, **1 hour (in-game)**, **25,000,000 ticks**`  
`- **Demo Recording**: **5 seconds (real-life)**, **2 hours (in-game)**, **50,000,000 ticks**`  
`- **Track Finalization**: **7.5 seconds (real-life)**, **3 hours (in-game)**, **75,000,000 ticks**`

`This document provides a clear reference for the time and resources needed for each stage of content creation in the game.`
