`### Leasing Process in "Record Label Simulator"`

`#### Leasing from eyeriS Broadcasting Corporation`  
`1. **Upfront Payment**: The player leases a recording studio structure and pays the rent upfront for 4 years.`

`#### Lease Expiry Options`  
`1. **Automatic Renewal**:`   
   `- **Condition**: Player can choose to automatically renew the lease.`  
   `- **Requirement**: Pay upfront for the next 4 years upon lease expiry.`  
     
`2. **Lease Expiration**:`  
   `- **Unrenewed Lease**: If the player doesn't renew the lease, the recording studio structure becomes available.`  
   `- **AI Occupancy**: The structure can then be leased by one of the 7 AI Record Labels.`  
   `- **Demolition**: If the structure remains unoccupied for 2 years, it is demolished.`  
   `- **Lot Recycling**: The lot is used for a new structure that is in demand (e.g., a residential floor).`
