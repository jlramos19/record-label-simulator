Record Label Simulator \- Records (Awards) and Certifications (Milestones) for In-Game Achievements for finishing Quests

Records (Awards):

* Records are prestigious and competitive achievements earned by individual artists or entities, setting them apart. Examples include:  
  * Winning a Grammy-equivalent award in the game.  
  * Achieving the highest-ever tour attendance.

Certifications (Milestones):

* Certifications are markers for broader objectives and attainable accomplishments. Multiple artists or players can achieve them. Examples include:  
  * Selling 1 million album copies.  
  * Reaching \#1 on the charts with a song within the weekly chart updates.
