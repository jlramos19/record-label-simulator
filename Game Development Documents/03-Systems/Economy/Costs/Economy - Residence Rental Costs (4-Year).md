### **`Economy - Residence Rental Costs (4-Year)`**

`Here are the calculations for the cost of residence buildings over 4 years:`

`1. **Number of Members per Building**:`  
   `- 80 floors × 4 rooms per floor × 5 members per room = 1,600 members per building`

`2. **Monthly Cost per Building**:`  
   `- $1,800 per member × 1,600 members = $2,880,000 per month`

`3. **Total Cost over 4 Years**:`  
   `- Number of months in 4 years accounting for leap years: 48 months`  
   `- Total cost for 4 years = $2,880,000 per month × 48 months = $138,240,000`

`So, the total cost for a residence building over 4 years is $138,240,000. Let me know if there are any other details or calculations you'd like to add!`
