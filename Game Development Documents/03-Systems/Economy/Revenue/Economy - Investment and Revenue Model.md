### **`Economy - Investment and Revenue Model`**

**`Game Design Overview`**`:`

* **`Player Role`**`: The CEO (Marc Hedren)`  
* **`Act Role`**`: The artist or band`  
* **`Currencies`**`: Investments and revenue`  
* **`Structures`**`: Marketing Department, Concert Venues, Media Studio`  
* **`Units`**`: Capsules (1,000 community members each)`

### **`Investment and Revenue Example`**

#### **`Initial Investments`**

1. **`CEO Investments`**`:`  
   * **`Marketing (Billboards, Ads, Radio, Playlisting)`**`: $20,000`  
   * **`Concert Tour`**`: $15,000`  
   * **`Total CEO Investment`**`: $35,000`  
2. **`Act Investments`**`:`  
   * **`Promotions (Interviews, Live Performances)`**`: $10,000`  
   * **`Concert Tour`**`: $15,000`  
   * **`Total Act Investment`**`: $25,000`

#### **`Revenue Generation`**

1. **`Single Release`**`:`  
   * **`Price`**`: $0.69`  
   * **`Sales Goal`**`: 100,000 copies (100 capsules)`  
   * **`Revenue`**`: 100,000 * $0.69 = $69,000`  
2. **`Concert Tour`**`:`  
   * **`Total Revenue`**`: $100,000`  
3. **`Total Revenue`**`:`  
   * **`Combined Revenue from Singles and Concerts`**`: $69,000 + $100,000 = $169,000`

#### **`Investment Proportions`**

* **`Total Investment`**`: $35,000 (CEO) + $25,000 (Act) = $60,000`  
* **`CEO Investment Percentage`**`: $35,000 / $60,000 = 58.33%`  
* **`Act Investment Percentage`**`: $25,000 / $60,000 = 41.67%`

#### **`Profit Distribution`**

* **`Total Revenue`**`: $169,000`  
* **`CEO Share`**`: 58.33% of $169,000 = $98,594.70`  
* **`Act Share`**`: 41.67% of $169,000 = $70,405.30`

### **`In-Game Implementation`**

1. **`Structures and Actions`**`:`  
   * **`Marketing Department`**`: Player allocates funds for marketing.`  
   * **`Concert Venues`**`: Both CEO and Act invest in promotions.`  
   * **`Media Studio`**`: Act pays for interviews and performances.`  
2. **`Gameplay Mechanics`**`:`  
   * **`Investment Tracking`**`: Monitor CEO and Act investments in various promotions.`  
   * **`Revenue Calculation`**`: Automate revenue from sales and concerts.`  
   * **`Profit Sharing`**`: Split profits based on investment percentages.`  
3. **`Example Scenario`**`:`  
   * `Player (CEO) invests heavily in marketing.`  
   * `Act focuses on live performances and interviews.`  
   * `Both invest equally in a concert tour.`  
   * `Revenue from single sales and concerts is split according to their respective investments.`
