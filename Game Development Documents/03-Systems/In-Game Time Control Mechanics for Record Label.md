Title: "In-Game Time Control Mechanics for Record Label Simulator"

- Pause: Halts simulation time while the player manages UI tasks.
- Play: Normal speed, 1 in-game hour every ~2.5 real seconds.
- Fast: Accelerated speed, 1 in-game hour every ~1 real second.
- Quick skips: 24h and 7d buttons jump forward immediately.
- Skip Time modal: Presets for 24h, 7d, 14d, 28d; custom day/hour skip; and skip-to-date/time (UTC).
- Long skips show a progress overlay and can be canceled mid-run.
