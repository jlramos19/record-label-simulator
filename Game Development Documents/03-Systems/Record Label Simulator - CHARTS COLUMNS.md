# **`Record Label Simulator - CHARTS COLUMNS DEFINITION`**

1. `#: This is the rank of the song on the chart for the current week. Itâ€™s usually ordered from 1 to whatever number represents the total number of songs on the chart, with 1 being the highest rank.`  
2. `Song: This is the title of the song.`  
3. `Recorder(s): This is the name of the individual artist, band, DJ, or other musical act who performed the song.`  
4. `RL (Record Label): This represents the record label that the creator(s) is signed to.`  
5. `LW (Last Week): This is the rank of the song on the chart for the previous week. It helps to track the movement of the song on the chart over time.`  
6. `Peak: This is the highest rank the song has reached on the chart since its release.`  
7. `WOC (Weeks on Chart): This is the total number of weeks the song has been on the chart.`  
8. `Sales: This represents how many song copies were sold that week.`  
9. `Streaming: This represents how often the song has been streamed online that week.`  
10. `Airplay: This represents how often the song has been played on radio stations that week.`  
11. `Social: This represents how often the song was used as background audio in social videos that week.`
