# **RLS Awards System**

## **1\. Overview**

This document defines the award structures for Record Label Simulator. It reflects finalized terminology, category structure, and quality systems.

## **2\. Award Show Families**

There are three major award families:

• Praised Content Awards (Praised CA) — Critics-based

• Pop Content Awards (Pop CA) — Audience/popularity-based

• Promotional Content Awards (Promo CA) — Promotional content only

## **3\. Quality Terminology**

Correct terms:

• Critics Rating (CR)  
• Audience Rating (AR)

Final Quality (FQ) \= Preliminary Quality (PQ) \+ Critics Rating (CR) \+ Audience Rating (AR).

## **4\. Promotional Content Awards (Promo CA)**

Promo CA focuses strictly on promotional content created in the game world. Interview-based and eyeriSocial-based categories are removed.

### **4.1 Eligible Promotional Content Types**

• Music Videos  
• Live Performances  
• Audience Dynamics (fanbases)

### **4.2 Category Structure (9 Total)**

#### ***A. Music Video Awards***

• Music Video of the Year (Hybrid: Pop \+ Praised)

• Most-Engaged Music Video (Pop)

• Critics’ Music Video Award (Praised)

#### ***B. Live Performance Awards***

• Live Performance of the Year (Hybrid)

• Most-Attended Live Performance (Pop)

• Critics’ Live Performance Award (Praised)

#### ***C. Audience Awards***

• Audience of the Year (Hybrid)

• Most Loyal Audience (Praised — based on highest-quality promotional content)

• Most Active Audience (Pop — engagement-based)

## **5\. Praised Content Awards (Praised CA)**

Praised CA categories remain aligned with critics-focused evaluation standards.

## **6\. Pop Content Awards (Pop CA)**

Pop CA categories remain aligned with audience engagement and popularity metrics.

## **7\. Terminology Corrections Applied**

• “Finished Content” → “Main Content”  
• “Unfinished Content” → “Preliminary Content”  
• “Review” → “Rating”  
• “Record Label” → “Record Label”

## **8\. Prestige Order**

1\. Praised CA  
2\. Pop CA  
3\. Promo CA
