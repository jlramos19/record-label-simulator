# **Record Label Simulator – Chart Evolution Framework**

## **Purpose**

This document defines the five **Game Time Stages** of chart evolution in **Record Label Simulator** and the rules that govern how **content performance** is calculated across **sales**, **streaming**, and **radio**.

**Era Stages** and **Game Time Stages** are separate systems; only **Game Time Stages** determine chart formulas.

---

## **1\. Game Time Stages Overview**

### **Game Time Stage 1 – Physical Age**

**Chart components enabled**

* Physical Sales only

* Radio Plays (high importance)

**Dominant format:** Physical  
 **Purpose:** Establishes the earliest, simplest unit-based system.

---

### **Game Time Stage 2 – Digital Transition**

**Chart components enabled**

* Physical Sales

* Digital Sales

* Radio Plays (medium-high importance)

**Dominant format:** Physical → Digital parity  
 **Purpose:** First layer of complexity as digital begins contributing.

---

### **Game Time Stage 3 – Digital Dominance**

**Chart components enabled**

* Digital Sales (high weight)

* Physical Sales (low weight)

* Radio (medium-low)

* Early Streams (low)

**Dominant format:** Digital  
 **Purpose:** Downloads become the primary unit generator.

---

### **Game Time Stage 4 – Streaming Age**

**Chart components enabled**

* Streams (medium weight)

* Digital Sales (medium)

* Physical (very low)

* Radio (low)

**Dominant format:** Streaming  
 **Purpose:** Streaming overtakes all sales.

---

### **Game Time Stage 5 – Streaming Fragmentation**

**Chart components enabled**

* Paid Streams (high)

* Free Streams (medium-low)

* Digital Sales (medium)

* Physical (niche-low)

* Radio (very low)

**Dominant format:** Paid Streaming  
 **Purpose:** Streaming splits into tiers of value.

---

## **2\. Format Weights per Stage**

### **Stage 1 – Physical Age**

* Physical: 1.0

* Radio: 0.75

### **Stage 2 – Digital Transition**

* Physical: 0.7

* Digital: 1.0

* Radio: 0.5

### **Stage 3 – Digital Dominance**

* Digital: 1.0

* Physical: 0.3

* Radio: 0.4

* Streams: 0.2

### **Stage 4 – Streaming Age**

* Streams: 1.0

* Digital: 0.6

* Physical: 0.1

* Radio: 0.2

### **Stage 5 – Streaming Fragmentation**

* Paid Streams: 1.0

* Free Streams: 0.4

* Digital: 0.6

* Physical: 0.1

* Radio: 0.1

---

## **3\. Chart Power Formula**

A track’s weekly chart score is calculated using the following formula:

**ChartPower \= Σ(ActivityAmount × FormatWeight × QualityModifier × ContentGenreAlignmentSynergy)**

Where:

* **ActivityAmount** \= units from sales, streams, or radio

* **FormatWeight** \= defined by the active Game Time Stage

* **QualityModifier** \= (Final Quality / 100\)

* **ContentGenreAlignmentSynergy** \= multiplier derived from **alignment** fit and preferred **content genre** fit

* **Promotional Boost** \= a separate additive system layered after the base score

---

## **4\. Notes**

* **Era Stages** (Direction, Creation, Promotion, Legacy) do not affect chart rules.

* **Game Time Stages** exclusively determine chart formula complexity and format weighting.
