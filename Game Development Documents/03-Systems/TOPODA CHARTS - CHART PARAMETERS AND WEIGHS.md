# **`Music Chart Weights:`**

## Base weights (default / fallback)

- Sales: 30% (Digital & Vinyl)
- Streaming: 30% (eyeriSocial Music, parody of streaming service)
- Airplay: 30% (Radio, clubs, public venues)
- eyeriSocial: 10% (counts how many times the song was used as background audio in videos uploaded to the social media Label)

## Regional consumption weights (used for regional charts)

- Annglora Capital: Sales 28% | Streaming 32% | Airplay 25% | Social 15%
- Annglora Elsewhere: Sales 35% | Streaming 24% | Airplay 31% | Social 10%
- Bytenza Capital: Sales 20% | Streaming 40% | Airplay 20% | Social 20%
- Bytenza Elsewhere: Sales 25% | Streaming 35% | Airplay 25% | Social 15%
- Crowlya Capital: Sales 25% | Streaming 25% | Airplay 35% | Social 15%
- Crowlya Elsewhere: Sales 32% | Streaming 20% | Airplay 33% | Social 15%

## Rollups

- National charts blend capital + elsewhere using the capital population share.
- Global chart blends national weights using the current population split.
