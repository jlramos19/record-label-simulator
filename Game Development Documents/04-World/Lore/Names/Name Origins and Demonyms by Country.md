* # Name Origin, Ethnicity, Culture, and Demonyms of countries in Record Label Simulator

*   
* Annglora:  
  * Name Origin: The "N" was added for clarity in pronunciation. From the term “Anglo” \+ the term “Flora”.  
  * Culture: The country is focused on flora.   
  * Ethnicity: The population predominantly resembles native North American and European individuals. Each inhabitant resides in their respective native continent within the fictional world, for the most part.  
  * Demonym: Anngloran  
  * Language: English  
* Bytenza:  
  * Name Origin: Initially named Beomguk, it was later refined to Bytenza, emphasizing the technological aspect with "byte." Finally, it became Bytenza for simplification and better pronunciation.  
  * Ethnicity: Bytenza is inhabited by people native to Asia and Oceania.  
  * Culture: Mostly neutral Alignment preference.  
  * Demonym: Bytenese  
  * Language: Korean  
* Crowlya:  
  * Name Origin: Originally based on the fact that its people are native African and South American, focusing on minerals, crystals, and gems. Crowlya’s original name was Crowlya.   
  * Ethnicity: Features native African and South American people.  
  * Culture: Crowlya is inspired by regal elements and risky Alignment.   
  * Demonym: Crowlish  
  * Language: Spanish 	

NOTE: emphasis on NATIVE ethnicities from those continents.
