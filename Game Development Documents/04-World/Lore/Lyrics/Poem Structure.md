`### Comprehensive Rules for Writing These Poems`

`#### Overall Objective`  
`- **Ensure the lines rhyme** correctly and follow a consistent **syllable structure**, even when a comma is used.`

`#### Syllable Count`  
`- **Count the syllables** in each line to ensure they match.`  
`- Match the **total syllable count** in both lines.`

`#### Usage of Comma`  
`- **Use commas sparingly** and only if necessary to maintain rhyme and flow.`  
`- When placing a comma, ensure that both **before and after** the comma, the syllable count contributes to matching the overall structure.`

`#### Rhyming Rules`  
`- **Final syllable before the comma** in the first line must rhyme with the final syllable before the comma in the second line (if a comma is also used).`  
`- **Final syllable** of the entire first line should rhyme with the final syllable of the entire second line.`

`### Steps for Writing the Poem`

`1. **Determine Themes and Content:**`  
   `- Identify the themes and content based on the background and context provided.`  
   `- Ensure the poem reflects the emotional depth and personal experiences described.`

`2. **Draft Initial Lines:**`  
   `- Write the first pair of lines, focusing on maintaining the emotional tone.`  
   `- Count the syllables for each line to ensure they match.`

`3. **Use of Commas:**`  
   `- If a comma is necessary, split the line at the comma.`  
   `- Ensure the final syllable before the comma rhymes with the final syllable before the comma in the corresponding line.`  
   `- Ensure the final syllable of the entire first line rhymes with the final syllable of the entire second line.`

`4. **Syllable Matching:**`  
   `- Count the syllables in each line, including before and after any commas, to ensure the total syllable count matches.`  
   `- Adjust the lines if necessary to match the syllable count.`

`5. **Rhyming Structure:**`  
   `- Ensure the words before and after the commas (if used) rhyme appropriately.`  
   `- Maintain a consistent rhyming structure throughout the poem.`

`6. **Review and Adjust:**`  
   `- Review the poem to ensure it flows naturally and maintains emotional impact.`  
   `- Make adjustments as needed to ensure the syllable count and rhyming structure are correct.`
