# **`Record Label Simulator — Country Parameters for Image Prompts`**

### **`Purpose`**

`Defines standardized parameters for generating Creator imagery prompts in Record Label Simulator. Provides visual, cultural, and stylistic anchors for each of the three countries: Annglora, Bytenza, and Crowlya.`

---

## **`General Parameters`**

* `Ethnic Backgrounds: Asian & Middle Eastern · White & Native · Black & Brown`

* `Lighting Colors: Red · Cyan · Green · Magenta · Gold`

* `Gender: Male · Female · Nonbinary`

* `Music Genres: Popular · Indie · Underground · (extend as needed)`

* `Settings: Forest · Stadium · TV show · Radio interview · (extend as needed)`

* `Professions: Dancer · Singer · Rapper · Recorder · Artist · (all music industry roles)`

* `Hair Length: Short · Medium · Long`

* `Hair Texture: Wavy · Curly · Straight · Coily`

* `Hair Color: Ginger · Blond · Black · Brown · Gray`

* `Eye Color: Green · Blue · Brown · Black · Hazel`

`Guidelines:`

* `Add “futuristic” to all prompts once.`

* `Portraits should be head-and-shoulder compositions.`

* `Environments should indicate wealth level (e.g., luxury hall vs. humble stage).`

* `Lighting can reflect age (glossy = youth, matte = older) and alignment (bright = positive, dark = negative).`

---

## **`Country Profiles`**

### **`Annglora`**

* `Theme & Identity: Flora · Environmentally focused`

* `Cultural Identity: Environmental preservation, spiritual devotion`

* `Dominant Color: Yellow (visualized as green in imagery)`

* `Language: English`

* `Key Location: Lilypad Lake, Bloomville (capital)`

* `Beliefs: Medium emphasis on supernatural and magic`

* `Economy: Low cost of living · Strong currency · Resource-based`

* `Geography: Predominantly north (capital in the south)`

* `Population: Large · Strongly religious · Liberal politics`

* `Acceptance: Legal & accepting of homosexuality`

* `Relations: Threatened by Bytenza · Allied with Crowlya`

* `Artists: Reflect flora/environmental focus (green light motifs)`

* `Demonym: Anngloran`

---

### **`Bytenza`**

* `Theme & Identity: Fauna · Power-driven`

* `Cultural Identity: Cultural mosaic · Governance & authority focus`

* `Dominant Color: Blue (visualized as cyan in imagery)`

* `Language: Korean`

* `Beliefs: Low emphasis on supernatural`

* `Economy: Medium cost of living · Balanced currency · Strong political/economic expertise`

* `Geography: Far east · Predominantly northern region`

* `Population: Medium · Nonreligious · Moderate politics`

* `Acceptance: Conservative stance on homosexuality`

* `Relations: Friendly with Crowlya · Threatens Annglora`

* `Artists: Reflect fauna/power themes (cyan lighting)`

* `Demonym: Bytenese`

---

### **`Crowlya`**

* `Theme & Identity: Regal · Technological advancement`

* `Cultural Identity: Blend of tech and Caribbean-inspired regality`

* `Dominant Color: Red (visualized as gold in imagery)`

* `Language: Spanish`

* `Key Location: Campana City (capital)`

* `Beliefs: High emphasis on destiny, zodiac, supernatural`

* `Economy: High cost of living · Weak currency · Tech-based, conservative values`

* `Geography: Central · Predominantly southern region`

* `Population: Small · Moderately religious · Conservative politics`

* `Acceptance: Mixed tolerance · Masked representation of homosexuality`

* `Relations: Defends Annglora · Balanced relations with Bytenza`

* `Artists: Reflect regal/tech focus (gold lighting)`

* `Demonym: Crowlish`
