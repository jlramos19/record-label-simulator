`### Record Label Simulator Specifications:`

`#### Minimum Requirements:`  
`- **OS**: Windows 10 64 bit`  
`- **Processor**: Intel Core i5-7400 (2.10 GHz to 2.69 GHz, 4 cores)`  
`- **Memory**: 8 GB RAM`  
`- **Graphics**: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1650 with 4 GB VRAM`  
`- **DirectX**: Version 12`  
`- **Storage**: 20 GB available space`  
`- **Display**: 1920 x 1080 resolution`

`#### Recommended Requirements:`  
`- **OS**: Windows 10 64 bit | Windows 11`  
`- **Processor**: Intel Core i7-9700 or equivalent (higher clock speeds and more cores)`  
`- **Memory**: 16 GB RAM or higher`  
`- **Graphics**: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3060 with 8 GB VRAM`  
`- **DirectX**: Version 12`  
`- **Storage**: 20 GB available space`  
`- **Display**: 3840 x 2160 (4K resolution) for 16:9 aspect ratio`
