Record Label Simulator \- Game Overview  
Game Overview

Concept: Record Label Simulator is an innovative strategy and simulation game where players take on the role of the CEO of a record label in a world shared by human and AI characters. As CEOs, players nurture artists to success while managing ‘FLAMP’ factors \- FREEDOM, Love, Ambition, MORALITY, and Power. Central to the game are the dynamics of three competing factions the player can choose from. The players' challenges and progress are tied not just to their individual actions and decisions but also to the faction they belong to.

content Genre: The game blends elements of the simulation, strategy, and micromanagement content genres to create a distinct gaming experience.

Target Audience: While specific demographic details haven't been provided, given the game's focus on strategy, decision-making, and music industry elements, it likely appeals most to gamers who enjoy nuanced strategic gameplay.

Game Flow Summary: Players begin by selecting a faction to join and start managing their record label with a selected number of artists. As they guide their artists to success, they earn 'Lead Sheets' that get added to their inventory. Simultaneously, balancing the 'FLAMP' factors contributes towards their overall game progression. The game continues in a cycle of growth, strategy, and balance between competing factions, achieving artist success, and managing 'FLAMP' factors.
