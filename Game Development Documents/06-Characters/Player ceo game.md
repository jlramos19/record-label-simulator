The stakes are how things will be measured, the ceo will unlock new things when reaching certain milestones like albums and singles sold, concert tickets sold, amount of streams, and other progress tracking metrics.   
The CEO will have stakes that will present what the CEO can do. 

In the Stake 1, the player is only expected to release a single and sell a total of x singles to unlock the next stake

In stake 2, the ceo is expected to release an album, and sell x albums to unlock stake 3\. 

In stake 3, the ceo is expected to release a music video and have it be purchased/streamed x amount of times before unlocking stake 4\. 

In stake 4, the ceo is expected to have a creator give an interview about the album or single, the interviews must reach x amount of viewers/readers to unlock stake 5

In the final stake, which is still undetermined, the player must reach certain milestones in the Record Label (record label). 

Basically, the game will progress and certain milestones must be met within the game to unlock the next stake.
