The premise is centered around discovering struggling creators dealing with negative emotions and helping them overcome those negative emotions and turn them into positive ones. This will be done by finding creators and having them create Sheet Music that can then be turned into Tracks, studio albums, and concert tours. The CEO decides if he wants to keep working with the CREATOR CARD once the era is completed.

Songs sent to streaming services are available for 50 in-game years.  
Songs and Album Sales in-game will be available for up to 10 billion.  
Airplay is limited to 10 billion as well.
