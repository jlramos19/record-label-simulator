`Community Member Theme and Mood Development`

	`1.	Inheritance:`

	`•	Community members inherit their first 4 slots from their parent members:`

	`•	2 preferred themes (dominant traits).`

	`•	2 preferred moods (recessive traits).`

	`•	These slots define the first 2 preferred content genres (1 theme and 1 mood per content genre).`

	`2.	0–3 Age Group (Consumption Phase):`

	`•	Community members only consume content during this stage.`

	`•	They gain their second preferred theme, adding a third slot to their theme pool.`

	`3.	20–23 Age Group (Completion Phase):`

	`•	Community members gain their second preferred mood, completing the final slot in their mood pool.`

	`•	At this stage, they have a total of 8 slots:`

	`•	4 preferred themes.`

	`•	4 preferred moods.`

	`4.	Result:`

	`•	By 23 years of age, community members have a complete set of 4 preferred content genres, combining 2 unique themes with 2 unique moods.`
