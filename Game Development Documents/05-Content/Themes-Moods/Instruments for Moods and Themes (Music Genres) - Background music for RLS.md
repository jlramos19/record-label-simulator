Each of the 45 music genres will have a distinct instrumental variation of the mood and theme it represents.   
For example, calming freedom can be slow-tempo quiet trumpets, the slow tempo representing calming, and the trumpets representing freedom. THIS WILL BE OPTIONAL IF THE PLAYER WANTS ACTIVE BACKGROUND MUSIC FEEDBACK. 

Themes:

FREEDOM: Trumpets  
LOYALTY: Saxophone  
AMBITION: Synthesizer  
MORALITY: Guitar  
POWER: Piano

Moods:

Cheering: Xylophone  
Saddening: Violin  
Thrilling: Cello  
Angering: Heavy Drums  
Calming: Flute  
Energizing: Drum Kit  
Uplifting: Music Box  
Boring: Not musically represented (negative mood)  
Daring: Not musically represented (uncertain mood)

This nuanced approach to the game's soundtrack and mood representation offers an intriguing way to guide player experience and narrative through music, selectively amplifying emotions and themes that drive engagement and immersion.
