#

`Pixel Art: This retro style hearkens back to the early days of computer graphics, where artists manipulate individual pixels to create images.`

`Vector Art: This style uses points, lines, curves, and shapes based on mathematical expressions to create artwork.`

`Glitch Art: Artists intentionally use digital or analog errors for aesthetic purposes.`

`Low Poly Art: This style is characterized by a low polygon count, often used in 3D models and animations.`

`Isometric Art: This style presents 3D objects in two dimensions, giving the art a unique, geometric aesthetic.`
