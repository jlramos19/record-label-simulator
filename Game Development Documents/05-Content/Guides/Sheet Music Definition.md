# **`Definition of what Sheet Music forms in the Record Label Simulator.`**

1. # **`Songs:`** 

   `Songs in your game are composed of one or more Sheet Music. They are the core musical units that players can produce, promote, and release. Songs vary in mood, theme, and content genre and are crucial in determining a player's success in the music industry.`

   2. # **`Albums:`** 

      `Albums are collections of bundled songs, often sharing thematic or stylistic connections. Players can group their songs into albums, creating cohesive bodies of work. Albums represent significant milestones in a musician's career and can impact their popularity and chart performance.`

   3. # **`Tours:`** 

      `Tours are live performances, or concert series artists can undertake to promote their music. They provide opportunities for artists to connect with their audience and generate revenue. Players can plan and manage tours, deciding on tour locations, setlists, and promotional strategies.`

   4. # **`Era (Album Cycle):`** 

      `An era, also known as an album cycle, represents a distinct phase in an artist's career characterized by the release and promotion of a specific album. It encompasses the period from the album's release to the start of the next album's production. During an era, artists promote their albums through various activities, including tours, music videos, and interviews. Eras define an artist's musical journey and evolution over time.`
