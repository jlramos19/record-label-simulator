# **Record Label Simulator RLS Pop and Cultural Instruments for Udio Prompts**

Purpose: Curated, pop- and culture-relevant instruments for RLS worldbuilding and fast Udio prompting. Each entry includes sonic descriptors, cultural tags, techniques, and a ready-to-use Udio prompt snippet.

## **Udio Prompt Pattern (quick)**

Mood/content Genre • Instrument Focus • Playing Technique • Tempo (BPM) • Mix/Mic Note • Culture/Region • Era • Language Vocals (if any) • Arrangement Shape

Example: “bright Latin pop with Dominican roots • focus on Dominican güira and tambora, tight merengue groove • 126 BPM • crisp percussion up-front, dry room • Santo Domingo street party • 2000s–2020s • Spanish crowd ad-libs • verse–chorus–bridge”
