# **Honorary Title: "Chameleon"**

In the world of Record Label Simulator, the ability to adapt to the dynamic music industry is not just a skill; it's an art. Acts that demonstrate extraordinary versatility earn the esteemed title of "Chameleon."

## **Criteria for Achievement:**

* **content** Genre Fluidity: Successfully navigate changes in music genres.  
* Alignment Management: Skillfully shift target Alignments amidst these changes.  
* Consistency: Maintain or improve the Act's success with each transition.  
* Frequency: Achieve the above criteria at least three times during the Act's career.

An Act distinguished with the "Chameleon" title is celebrated for its ability to reinvent itself while keeping the spotlight burning bright. This title does not come easily; it is reserved for those who have mastered the art of transformation and emerged victorious.
