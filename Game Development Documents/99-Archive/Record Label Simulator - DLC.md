Feature Pack: These packs introduce entirely new features to the game. You could use taglines like “Unleash New Possibilities”, “Experience the Unseen”, or “Discover New Dimensions”. This will clearly communicate that players will be getting something completely new to explore and enjoy.

Expansion Pack: These packs expand upon existing features. You could use taglines like “Dive Deeper”, “Expand Your Horizons”, or “Unlock the Full Potential”. This will let players know that they’ll be able to enjoy more of what they already love about the game.

Chapter Pack: These packs introduce, expand, or continue the lore of the game. You could use taglines like “Unfold the Saga”, “Continue the Epic”, or “Discover Hidden Lore”. This will signal to players that they’ll be getting more story content, which could include new characters, plotlines, and lore.
