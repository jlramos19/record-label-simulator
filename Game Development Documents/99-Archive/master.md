# **MASTER RECORDING (PRELIMINARY CONTENT)**

## **Overview**

The Master Recording is the final step of Preliminary Content in the Creation Pipeline. It is produced in the Mixing Studio by the Producer occupation. At this stage, the Demo is transformed into the finalized internal audio version before release.

## **Definition**

A Master Recording is the internal final audio file created after the Demo. It is still Preliminary Content because it has not yet been released as a Track.

This is the step where:  
\- the Content Genre is applied  
\- the Fusion Profile is applied (when applicable)  
\- Preliminary Quality is determined

Once released, the Master Recording becomes the Track.

## **Origin**

The Master Recording step exists to finalize the Theme (from Sheet Music) and Mood (from the Demo) into the Track’s content Genre. This is done by the Producer inside the Mixing Studio.

## **Usage**

\- Defines the Track’s content Genre  
\- Assigns the Preliminary Quality  
\- Finalizes the audio asset before public release  
\- Transitions the Content from “Preliminary” to “Main Content” (Track)

## **Related Concepts**

\- Sheet Music (Theme)  
\- Demo Recording (Mood)  
\- Producer (Creator occupation completing this step)  
\- Mixing Studio  
\- Track (the Master Recording once released)  
\- Preliminary Quality
