# **`eyeriS Broadcasting Corporation (eBC)`**

**`eBC (eyeriS Broadcasting Corporation)`** `is the monopoly multimedia outlet in "Record Label Simulator" that broadcasts Content to Community Members.`

 

1. **`eyeriSocial:`**  
   * `The umbrella company overseeing social interactions and community engagement within the game.`

2. **`eyeriSound:`**  
   * `The subcompany dedicated to streaming and buying songs, offering a comprehensive music experience.`  
     1. `eyeriSound Streaming is the subdivision solely focused on the streaming of tracks and projects.`

3. **`eyeriShow:`**  
   * `The subcompany focused on promoting concert in venues structures within the game.`

4. **`eyeriStock:`**  
   * `The subcompany handling virtual merchandise, allowing players to design and sell items related to the artists and their music.`

5. **`eyeriScene:`**  
   * `The subcompany specializing in broadcasting music video content, including artist performances and music videos.`

6. **`eyeriSpeak:`**  
   * `The subcompany providing text and video chat features, enhancing communication and interaction among players.`

7. **`eyeriStudy:`**  
   * `The subcompany where characters level up their skills. These skills can be: the 5 themes and 9 moods, the different speed.` 

8. **`eyeriStories:`**  
   * `The subcompany that posts news updates to the eyeriSocial feed.`

9. **`eyeriStats:`**  
   * `The subcompany that gathers and analyzes statistics and chart data.` 

10. **`eyeriStation:`**  
    * `The subcompany broadcasting tracks (like one would in a radio station) and interviews of acts’ current rollout strategy.`

11. **`eyeriShipping:`**  
    * `The subcompany where characters with the occupation to ‘distribute’ Content to Shopping Centers.` 

12. **`eyeriStudios:`**  
    * `The subcompany with a chain of studios with different purposes like recording and broadcast studios.`

13. **`eyeriScholars:`**  
    * `The subcompany with historians that restore previous information about the history of the humans on Earth.`

14. **`eyeriStorage:`**  
    * `The subcompany where characters with the occupation to ‘manage’ mass produces content.`
