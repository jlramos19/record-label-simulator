# **`Record Label Simulator - Gameplay Mechanic: Signing Artists (Acquiring CREATORS and (deprecated term) CARDS)`**

# **`In-Game CREATORS and (deprecated term) CARDS are acquired by the following methods:`**

##  **`By completing quests`**

When CEOs (Players) complete quests, they are awarded with special CREATORS and (deprecated term) cards.

## **`By signing CREATORS and renting (deprecated term).`**

CEOs have the option to sign CREATORS and rent (deprecated term) for a certain amount of turns.
