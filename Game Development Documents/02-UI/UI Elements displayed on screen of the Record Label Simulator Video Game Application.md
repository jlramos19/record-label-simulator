`UI Elements displayed on screen of the Record Label Simulator Video Game Application`

`# UI Slots`

`## Top Area`

`### Top Left Corner Area`

`- Displays the Portrait of the Player’s Record Label’s most successful Act in the last 6 Months.`

`### Top Left Area`

`#### Top Left's Left Area`

`- Popularity, Audience Reach and Alignment:`

`Popularity Position and Audience Percentage Covered (as in, reached, that the audience is actively aware of the Record Label and its acts and projects.)`

`#### Top Left's Right Area`

`- Wallet:`

`the Record Label’s available funds for spending in content creation. When clicked, opens a pop-up window over the gameplay area to display the player’s wallet and cards.`

`### Top Center Area`

`- Current In-game Time and Date:`

`The time is displayed and formatted as: “SAT - JAN 01, 2400 - 12AM” spaced out and in all-caps, and without quotation marks.`

`- Time Speed Controls:`

`- There are four buttons: Pause Game, Play Normal, Play Faster, Skip Time.`

`- Pause Game pauses the game and brings the time speed to 0.`

`- Play Normal progresses the in-game time at a rate of 1 in-game hour per 2.5 real-life seconds, or 420 real-life seconds (7 minutes) per in-game week.`

`- Play Faster progresses the in-game time at a rate of 1 in-game hour per 1 real-life second, or 168 real-life seconds (2:48 minutes) per in-game week.` 

`- Skip Time advances the game to the date the player decides. Presets for advancing the time 24 hours (1 day), 7 days (1 week), 14 days (2 weeks), and 28 days (4 weeks) are available. The player can also enter a custom in-game date or amount of time.`

`- View Navigation:`

`The top bar includes routed views so only one view is mounted at a time.`  
`Views: Charts (default), Create Content, Releases, Eras, Roster, Creator Community Chamber (CCC), eyeriSocial.`

`Creator Community Chamber (CCC) is a routed view for signing Creator IDs, scouting label standings, and trend watch.`

`### Top Right Area`

`#### Top Right’s Left Area`

`- Top Trends:`

`Displays the trending of the top 3 music genres, which is calculated by how present and high in the charts these music genres are, regardless of sales. (Shown in CCC view.)`

`#### Top Right’s Right Area`

`- Top Record Labels:`

`displays the Record Labels selling the most content. This is based on sales, streaming, and airplay of the content put out by the Record Label. (Shown in CCC view.)`

`### Top Right Corner Area`

`- SETTINGS:`

`Displays an icon that opens a window to see the settings of the application and in-game settings, like save and load game, and close application, exit to menu, resume game, etc.`

`## Left Side Area`

`### Left Side’s Top Area`

`- COMMUNITIES:`  
`displays four icons: areas, creators, items and tools, and collaborative content.`

`- Areas: Allows the Player to lease new area for usage within their Record Label operations.`

`- Creators: Allows the Player to find new Creators to sign to their Record Label in the 45 different in-game Communities.`

`- Items & Tools: Allows the Player to unlock Items & Tools that allow the creation of more Content. Items are equipped to Areas to create Content.`

`- Collabs: Allows the Player to have their Acts participate in the Eras of NPC Record Labels.` 

`NOTE: Collabs button in Communities offers the Player the chance to seek to collaborate with other Record Labels in the NPC’s Eras’ Projects. the collaborative content icon is to find opportunities to collaborate with the other 7 NPC Record Labels in content creation like track features for projects, etc.`

`### Left Side’s Bottom Area`

`- HARMONY HUB:`

`displays the same five icons, but this time the ones that the player’s Record Label owns:`

`both these ui elements open popup windows over the center’s top area opf the screen over the normal gameplay.`

`NOTE: Collabs button in Harmony Hub offers the Player the chance to seek to collaborate with other Record Labels in the Player’s owned Eras’ Projects.`

`## Center Area`

`### Center’s Top Area`

`- Gameplay Screen:`  
 `which is usually either the live charts or images and/or videos and/or animations to immerse the player in the game.`

`### Center’s Bottom Area`

`#### Center’s Bottom Right Area`

`- Quick Recipes:`  
`displays recipes for the player to learn what area needs what IDs to produce what content. When clicked, opens a window with all unlocked recipes over the gameplay screen area.`

`#### Center’s Bottom Center and Left Area`

`##### Center’s Bottom Center Area`

`- AREA BEING INTERACTED WITH BY THE PLAYER. For example: Studios, Venues, Advertising Spaces, etc.`

`##### Center’s Bottom Left Area`

`- PLAYER’S INVENTORY:`

`Contains the Record Label’s finished content that can be scheduled to be released and promoted.`

`## Right Side Area`

`### Right Side’s Top Area`

`- Quests:`

`Main and Side quests are given to the player by the CEO of the Record Label (main) about the protagonist’s lore (the CEO) and the communities in-game (side) about the in-game universe’s lore.`

`### Right Side’s Bottom Area`

`- Calendar:`

`displays the Record Label's internal schedule (Eras view) and upcoming releases. This displays 4 upcoming weeks. The player can click on the calendar to open a pop-up window over the gameplay screen to review Label/Public/Eras tabs and filters.`

`Calendar tabs show Label (internal), Public (world), and Eras views. Filters toggle scheduled vs released events and rival activity.`

`The **Player** can click on the Calendar UI Element to open the full calendar on top of the gameplay screen area to schedule content for release.`

`## Bottom Area`

`### Bottom Left Corner Area`

`- Promote Content Icon:`

`View the content released organized. Access the menu to choose how and when to promote content.`

`### Bottom Center Area`

`#### Bottom Center’s Left Area`

`- The portrait of the NPC character that the player is currently interacting with.`

`#### Bottom Center’s Center and Right`

`- The dialogue of the NPC character that the player is currently interacting with.`

`### Bottom Right Corner Area`

`- eyeriSocial icon:`  
`live world feed plus public announcements; the public only knows what the Label posts.`
